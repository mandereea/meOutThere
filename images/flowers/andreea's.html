The Five Elements theory states that the taste of hebal ingredients is a key determinant of their action in the body.

Bitter: ingredients tend to have a cleansing by clearing heat, drying dampness and promoting downward elimination

Sweet: ingredients tend to slow down acute reactions and detoxify. They also have a tonic effect - they replenish Qi and Blood.

Sour: ingredients help regulate internal movements and restrain abnormal discharges.

Salty: ingredients tend to have a draining effect in the body because they clear accumulations, remove phlegm and soften hard lumps.

Pungent: ingredients tend to promote Flow - the circulations of Qi and Fluids.
